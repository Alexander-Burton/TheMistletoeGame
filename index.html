The Leaf Game!

Play a game for science!

We are studying parasitic plants called Mistletoe.

Mistletoe attach to a host plant and extract water and nutrients from the host in order to survive!

Australia has many species of mistletoe. What is unusual is the number of mistletoe that appear to look like their host. We are investigating why this might be.

In this game you play as a hungry possum that sees a leaf and must decide which group of leaves it belongs to.

The data provided by everybody who plays this game will then be used to assess how Mistletoe's visual similarity to it's host plant relates to the presence of defensive chemicals.
