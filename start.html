How To Play:

Over 15 rounds, you will be shown a center leaf and two groups of three leaves from two different species.

You will then need to decide which of the two groups the center leaf is the same species as.

(You won't know which leaves are Mistletoe and which are not)

You will then have 10 seconds to decide which group the center leaf belongs to.

To make your decision, press the "Left Group" or "Right Group" buttons presented

You can also use your keyboard by pressing the Left and Right Keys on your keyboard
